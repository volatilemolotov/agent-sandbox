Terms are ANDed. metric:, source:, instance: match those fields; any other key:value term matches a label (verb:PATCH, le:+Inf, code:200); bare text matches anywhere; a value starting with = matches exactly. The query is the view: while it matches several metrics you get a list, and one matching metric shows its chart and series. Clicking a metric locks metric:=… into the query; clicking a series locks that whole series (and shows its raw samples) — back out by editing the query. Counter-style metrics (_total/_count/_sum/_bucket) plot as per-second rates by default — untick "rate" for raw cumulative values. Dashed blue lines mark phase starts.
